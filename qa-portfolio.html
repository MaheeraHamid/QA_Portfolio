About the Project

OnRekord is a blockchain-powered music rights and royalties platform that allows artists, producers, and contributors to bundle music assets (stems, tracks, videos) into SuperNFTs — a tamper-proof digital record of ownership and contribution on the blockchain.

The platform handles smart contract-driven royalty distributions, NFT minting and trading on a marketplace, USDC payment flows, KYC-linked identity verification, and real-time sync between on-chain events and the application UI. This is one of the most technically complex environments a QA engineer can work in — every bug can have financial consequences for real artists and rights holders.

I joined as the primary SQA engineer at the OnRekord App stage and have tested across every major product release through V1.1, covering the core app, the NFT marketplace, backend APIs, and smart contract integrations.
